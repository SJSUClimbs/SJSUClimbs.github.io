About Us

The Climbing Club of SJSU’s purpose is to help students discover the fun and active lifestyle the sport provides and foster a community around climbing. This purpose is to be achieved through fellowship in events and activities organized by the club and engage others that may be interested. We intend on reaching out to other climbing related institutions and organizations to create more of these opportunities.
